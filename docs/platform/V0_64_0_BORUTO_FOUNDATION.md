# v0.64.0 Boruto Benchmark Foundation

## Purpose

Establish the Boruto benchmark dataset.

This checkpoint creates the benchmark structure only.

No production implementation occurs during this version.

The benchmark follows the certified characterization framework introduced in
v0.63.6.

## Goals

- Create Boruto benchmark directories.
- Define benchmark scope.
- Identify representative benchmark episodes.
- Identify representative manga chapters.
- Prepare characterization.

## Scope

Anime

Boruto: Naruto Next Generations

Episodes

1–293

Manga

Boruto Chapters 1–80

## Initial Benchmark Episodes

The initial benchmark focuses on representative episodes covering:

- Beginning of series
- Canon adaptation
- Anime-original content
- Mixed adaptation
- Arc transitions
- Late-series adaptation

Episode numbers will be finalized during characterization.

## Manga Scope

Boruto Chapters

1–80

This benchmark introduces an independent manga dataset and does not reuse the
Naruto manga benchmark.

## Characterization Requirements

The Boruto benchmark must satisfy every requirement defined by:

V0_63_6_CHARACTERIZATION_TEMPLATE.md

## Certification

The Boruto benchmark will be considered certified after:

- Characterization complete
- Benchmark tests complete

- Frontend validation complete
